Sweet & Spicy Salmon Bowls

credit: @soleila_wellness on Instagram

Ingredients (makes 2 bowls)

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 ripe mangos
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/3 cup chopped coriander
  • 1 lime

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, add diced mangos, cucumber, and avocado
  2. Add finely chopped red onion and jalapeño as well as 1/3 cup of chopped coriander
  3. Add squeezed lime juice and mix
  4. In a different bowl, combine siracha, maple syrup, minced garlic, soy sauce, paprika, and salt
  5. Cut salmon fillet into 2 pieces and place on sheet of aluminum foil
  6. Spread seasoning over the top of each piece
  7. Cook salmon in airfryer for 8 minutes at 400 degrees fahrenheit
  8. Over a bed of white rice, portion out half of the mango salsa and 1 piece of salmon
